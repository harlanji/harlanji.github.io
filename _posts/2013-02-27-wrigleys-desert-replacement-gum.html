I have tried 2 of these now, and wow, A+ if you are not anti-gum.

I’ve tried apple crisp and now mint chocolate, both are stellar 2 minute bursts of “realistic” flavor. they’ll not replace the real thing for sure, but these could go SO FAR for people with an occasional sweet tooth.

I don’t think they can literally replace desert after a meal; but I think they are good for the occasional nights I wish I had sweets. these hit the spot!

I am of course weary of what kinds of mood altering chemicals might be in these things, but I’m not much less worried about commercially produced deserts from the freezer asile anyway. 

for now it seems to be a price worth paying, but when my weight stabilizes (or I am bulking) I will surely opt away from these artificial crutches.
